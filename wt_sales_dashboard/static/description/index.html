Overview

This Sales & Inventory Dashboard provides a real-time overview of sales performance and inventory status in Odoo. It combines KPIs, charts, and quick navigation to improve business insights.

Features

Interactive Sales & Inventory Dashboard with KPIs, charts, and tables.

Daily sales revenue for the last 30 days.

Displays the five best-selling products.

Recent 5 sales orders with quick drill-down to order forms.

Sales managers can drill down instantly into sales orders or product details without leaving the dashboard.

Configuration

  • KPI Cards

    • Total Sales

    This card displays the total revenue generated in the last 30 days and if you click on cards to show all orders.

    Average Order Value

    This card shows the average value of customer orders.

    Total Orders

    this card counts the number of confirmed sales orders and Click on card to show all orders.

    Low Stock Products

    This card shows products with available stock less than or equal to 10,product type is good and Track inventory is True and if you click on card then show low stock products.

  • Charts & Analytics

    • Sales Trend

    This card shows daily sales revenue for the last 30 days to helps track sales growth and seasonal patterns.

    Top 5 Selling Products

    This card displays the five best-selling products based on quantity sold to Helps identify top performers and demand trends..

  • Recent Sales Orders Table

    • This table displays the 5 most recent confirmed/done orders with details of Order Number,Customer Name,Date,Total Amount,Status and Clicking an order row directly opens the order form view.
